OpenGovIntelligence

Fostering Innovation and Creativity in Europe through Public Administration Modernization towards Supplying and Exploiting Linked Open Statistical Data

The vision of the OpenGovIntelligence project is to stimulate sustainable economic growth in Europe through fostering innovation in society and enterprises. Exploiting Linked Open Statistical Data through advanced data analytics and visualizations enables better-informed decision-making and allocation of limited resources. Towards this end, modernization of Public Administration with new processes, policies, and technologies should be achieved to enable the co-production of innovative data-driven public services.

Coordinating person: Prof. Konstantinos Tarabanis, Centre for Research & Technology - Hellas (CERTH) (Email kat AT uom.gr)
